Our Story

About CP Matrix

CP Matrix is a complete range of premium architectural coatings crafted with advanced technology to deliver superior performance, long-lasting beauty and ultimate protection.

By HD Origin Private Limited, CP Matrix was built on the belief that every home, office, and building deserves the highest quality paint — one that protects, endures, and inspires. Our technical team constantly innovates, ensuring our formulations are suited for India's demanding climate.

From entry-level interior emulsions to flagship premium ranges, from exterior weather shields to damp-proof primers, the CP Matrix portfolio is designed to offer a complete solution for every surface and every budget.

Backed by 200+ dealers, trusted by 500+ projects, and built on 12 years of excellence, CP Matrix continues to deliver strength, style, and protection to homes and buildings across Tamil Nadu and beyond.
