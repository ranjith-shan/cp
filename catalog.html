Our Story

About Ranjith Paints

Founded in 2005, Ranjith Paints has been Chennai's most trusted paint specialist for over two decades. What began as a single showroom in Anna Nagar has grown into Tamil Nadu's premier destination for premium interior, exterior, and specialty paints.

We believe that colour transforms spaces and that every home, office, and building deserves paints of the highest quality — paints that protect, endure, and inspire. Our technical team constantly innovates, ensuring our formulations meet global standards while being perfectly suited for India's climate.

With over 2,000 shades available across our ranges, our tinting technology ensures you get the precise colour you envision. Our certified colour consultants offer free in-home advice, and our network of trained applicators guarantees flawless results on every project.

From the smallest apartment repaint to large commercial developments, Ranjith Paints delivers the same commitment to quality, service, and lasting value that has made us the painter's first choice across South India.
